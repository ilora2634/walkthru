Personal Interests

One of my personal interests is running. I run varsity track at Issaquah High School and my three events are the 4x400m, 400m, and 800m. Last year my 4x400 team and I placed 5th in the state and are hoping for another amazing season. I love running because it has brought an amazing community of people into my life and has taught me many lessons along the way as well as bring some of my favorite memories into my life.


One event that was amazing was Oregon Relays where we traveled to the University of Oregon to compete. I ran the 4x400m and the 400 leg of the sprint medley relay. It was such an amazing experience and helped me get better as a runner.
